# The Everlasting Purple Node

![Everlasting Purple Node](everlasting_purple_node_finished_room_v3.png)

## Overview

The **Everlasting Purple Node** is an open-source, public domain lightbulb design that fundamentally reimagines lighting technology. It combines an electrodeless plasma discharge system with a Dysprosium-doped (Dy³⁺) borosilicate glass envelope to produce a deep 400–415nm violet-purple glow. 

This is not just a light source; it is a passive, continuous environmental health device designed to fit into a standard E26 socket.

### Key Features

*   **Essentially Infinite Lifespan:** Operates via a 250kHz resonant field exciting an argon plasma column. With no filament to burn out and no electrodes to erode, the core components (including a Zirconia-Yttria ceramic lattice) are practically indestructible.
*   **Continuous Environmental Sterilization:** The 400–415nm violet light destroys airborne and surface pathogens (including MRSA, COVID-19, influenza, and fungi) via the porphyrin-ROS photochemical mechanism, while remaining completely safe for continuous human, animal, and plant exposure.
*   **Endocannabinoid System (ECS) Activation:** Direct photobiomodulation at this wavelength has been shown to positively modulate CB1 and CB2 receptors, promoting natural analgesia, reduced inflammation, and elevated mood without psychotropic effects.
*   **Pineal Gland & Neuroendocrine Support:** The violet spectrum penetrates transcranially to stimulate OPN5 (Neuropsin) receptors in the hypothalamus, optimizing melatonin production, priming the serotonin-tryptamine pathway, and supporting deep, restorative sleep.
*   **Telomere Protection:** By optimizing melatonin and reducing oxidative stress (ROS) and inflammaging, the light supports telomerase upregulation, helping to slow cellular aging and maintain tissue health.
*   **Standard Form Factor:** All driving electronics (GaN power IC, E65 ferrite transformer, 50F supercapacitor, boost converter) are housed within a CNC-machined 6063 aluminum finned chassis that screws into any standard E26 socket.

## Repository Contents

This repository contains all the necessary files to understand, build, and modify the Everlasting Purple Node:

*   `everlasting_purple_node_GUIDE.md`: The master design document detailing the theory of operation, assembly steps, and safety protocols.
*   `everlasting_purple_node_CONFIG.json`: The 3D coordinate mapping and physical dimensions of all internal components.
*   `everlasting_purple_node_PARTS.csv`: The complete bill of materials (BOM) required for construction.
*   `everlasting_purple_node_MECHANICAL_CONNECTIONS.json`: The structural relationship and mounting hierarchy of the components.
*   `everlasting_purple_node_visual_aid.png`: A photorealistic cutaway diagram showing the internal components.
*   `everlasting_purple_node_finished_room_v3.png`: A photorealistic render of the finished bulb in use.
